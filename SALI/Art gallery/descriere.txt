In Vienna's Ungargasse lies Galerie Taith, a ground-floor commercial space with barrier-free access. A beautifully spacious 75m2 room with artworks on the blue-gray walls. Changing exhibitions!
Controllable LED lighting, projector, flipchart and various furniture make the location interesting for meetings, seminars, workshops, yoga and dinners.
